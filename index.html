Personal Website Plan

Project Name

Arachnids and the Environment — an educational project exploring how arachnids contribute to ecological balance and biodiversity.

Site Title

“Arachnids: Nature’s Engineers”

Target Audience

Students, nature enthusiasts, and environmental advocates. Anyone seeking accessible information about arachnids’ environmental roles or people interested in their benefits and characteristics.

Site Purpose

The purpose of the site is to educate and reframe perspectives on arachnids by showing their importance. Arachnids are often feared or ignored, yet they play a vital role in maintaining healthy environments.

Site Topics

  • Identification guides for major true arachnid groups (spiders, scorpions, mites, harvestmen, etc.)
  • Ecological roles
  • Myth‑debunking section
  • Conservation and biodiversity roles
  • Fun facts

Comparable Sites

  • https://arachnology.org — The British Arachnological Society’s site offering professional research and species data on arachnids.
  • https://spiders.us — A U.S. spider identification and education resource for students and hobbyists.
  • https://insectidentification.org — A broad insect and arachnid ID site with visual guides and regional species information for beginners.

Main Features / Content

  • Home section with intro and overview
  • Species pages with interactive ID cards (images + facts)
  • Myth vs. reality section
  • Food web and ecosystem impact section
  • Photo gallery
  • Links to scientific research

Site Personality

Educational but engaging. The site should invoke curiosity and empathy. Layouts and color schemes will be used to shape the emotional response.

Accessibility Information

  • High‑contrast text with readable fonts
  • Alt text for all images and diagrams
  • Clear focus indicators
  • Descriptive link text
  • Responsive layout for mobile and tablet users
